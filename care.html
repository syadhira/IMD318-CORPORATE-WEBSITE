How to Care for Your Perfume

Store Your Perfume Properly
To keep your perfume lasting longer, store it in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight.
Keep the Bottle Closed
Always keep the bottle tightly closed to avoid exposure to air, which can change the fragrance.
Avoid Spraying on Clothing
Perfume is best applied to your skin, as it blends with your body’s natural oils.
Don’t Over Apply
Less is more. A few spritzes on pulse points (wrists, neck) are enough to create a lasting fragrance.
