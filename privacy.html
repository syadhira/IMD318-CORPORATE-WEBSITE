Privacy & Policy

At Euphoria Essence, your privacy is our priority. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect personal details such as your name, email address, and phone number when you sign up for updates, place an order, or contact us. We may also gather non-personal information like your browser type and site usage through cookies.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Improve our products and services
  • Send promotional materials and updates (with your consent)
  • Respond to your queries and support requests

Protecting Your Information

We implement robust security measures to ensure your personal data is protected. This includes encryption, secure servers, and limited access to sensitive information.

Your Choices

You can opt out of receiving promotional emails or modify your cookie preferences at any time. Contact us if you wish to update or delete your personal data.

Changes to This Policy

Euphoria Essence reserves the right to update this Privacy & Policy document. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
